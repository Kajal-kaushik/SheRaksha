## 🚨 Problem Statement

Women often feel unsafe while traveling, especially during late hours or in unfamiliar areas.  
Many harassment cases go unreported due to fear, shame, or identity exposure.  

There is also a lack of awareness about government and NGO safety resources.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

**SheRaksha** is a **context-aware women safety platform** that:
- Suggests **safe travel routes** 🗺️  
- Provides **real-time safety insights** 📊  
- Enables **anonymous reporting** 🚨  

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🗺️ Safe Route Navigation
- Recommends safest routes instead of shortest  
- Based on time + crowd + safety data  

### 🧠 Context-Aware Intelligence
- Smart decision making using:
  - Location 📍  
  - Time ⏰  
  - Crowd conditions 👥  

### 🚨 Anonymous Complaint System
- Secure and private reporting  
- Encourages users to speak up  

### ⚠️ Smart Alerts
- Detects unsafe zones  
- Suggests safer alternatives
